# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

PolyTalk contributors are expected to keep discussion respectful, technical, and
focused on improving the project.

Examples of expected behavior:

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect different experience levels and deployment constraints.
- Give actionable feedback with enough context to reproduce or verify it.
- Accept constructive criticism and keep discussions on the work.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Derailing technical discussions with repeated off-topic comments.
- Sharing credentials, private transcripts, or customer data in public threads.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, merge requests, or other
contributions that violate this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations
may result in temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.

Report conduct concerns to:

```text
security@bizzappdev.com
```
